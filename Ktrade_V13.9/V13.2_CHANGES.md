# KTrade — V13.2 (validation-feedback fixes)

Small, correct, fully-tested responses to two validation passes. Plus one
correction: an auditor reported the desync→kill wire-up and the VIX conservative
fallback as "still missing" — they are NOT. Both shipped in V12.8 and are verified
present in this build (`_broker_truth_guard()` is defined and CALLED in
`run_autonomous`; `_vix_is_stale()` gates new entries). That audit diffed against
the wrong baseline. No duplicate logic was added.

## Fixed
1. **Size-weighted veto benefit (the most important fix).** The ledger now logs
   `qty`, `notional`, and `dollar_risk`, and `copilot_analysis.py` reports a
   **notional-weighted** net veto benefit (in $) alongside the equal-weighted one
   (pct-pts). This matters because a wrong veto on a large trade can outweigh
   several correct vetoes on small ones — the equal-weighted view can say "vetoes
   helped" while the dollar view says they lost money. When the two disagree in
   sign, the verdict now says so explicitly and tells you to trust the dollar
   figure. (Demonstrated in the test: equal-wt +4.00 pct-pts but $-wt **-$200**.)

2. **UTC ledger timestamps.** Ledger `ts` is now `datetime.now(timezone.utc)` so
   forward-return scoring is correct across servers/timezones. The analyzer already
   parses tz-aware ISO timestamps.

3. **RUN_TESTS.cmd refreshed.** Now runs the full current suite (v103…v131 +
   price-sanity) under a clean `KTRADE_APP_DATA_DIR`, instead of three old tests.
