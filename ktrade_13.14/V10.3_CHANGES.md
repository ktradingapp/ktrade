  (the KLAC $2300.52 decimal-shift tick is blocked before sizing).
- `RiskEngine` tracks real open positions (`record_fill`, `sync_positions`) and
  real equity (`update_equity`); MAX-POSITIONS / exposure checks are live again.
- `record_fill()` runs only after a confirmed broker fill.
- Real Alpaca **paper** bracket execution via NEW `agent/broker_adapter.py`,
  gated by `KTRADE_PAPER_ORDER_SUBMISSION=true` (off by default).
- ORB only contributes on intraday data; daily weights renormalised.
- Daily loss guard uses real broker equity.
- `RiskEngine` is the sole position sizer (`desired_risk_dollars`).
- Cost optimizer counts one call per ticker (`record_calls`).
- Market hours are `America/New_York` aware with weekend + 2026 holiday checks.
